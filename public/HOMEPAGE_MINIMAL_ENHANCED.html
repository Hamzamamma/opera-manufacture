Frequently asked questions

Everything you need to know

Do I need to pay any upfront costs?

No! Opera Manufacture is completely free to use. You only pay for production costs when a customer places an order. There are no monthly fees, setup fees, or hidden charges.

How long does shipping take?

Most orders ship within 2-3 business days after production. Delivery times vary by location but typically take 3-7 days domestically and 7-14 days internationally.

What file formats do you accept?

We accept PNG, JPG, SVG, and PDF files. For best print quality, we recommend uploading high-resolution images (at least 2000x2000px for raster images).

Can I see samples before selling?

Yes! You can order samples at cost price to check quality before selling to customers. We also provide instant digital mockups for all products.

How do I get paid?

When a customer orders, you receive your profit margin directly to your connected payment account. Payouts are processed weekly via PayPal, Stripe, or bank transfer.

What if a customer has an issue?

Our customer support team handles all order issues, returns, and refunds. You can focus on creating while we take care of customer service.
